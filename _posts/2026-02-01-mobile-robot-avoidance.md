---
title: Learning-Based Pedestrian Avoidance for Mobile Robots
description: Reflections from reinforcement learning work on mobile robot navigation around people.
---

Pedestrian avoidance is a core problem for robots that operate near people. The difficult part is not simply avoiding static obstacles, but reacting to irregular and dynamic human motion while preserving useful robot behavior.

My master's work on deep reinforcement learning-based pedestrian avoidance shaped my research direction toward robot learning systems that can handle uncertainty in real environments.
