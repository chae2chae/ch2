This master's thesis studied how mobile robots can avoid pedestrians and irregularly moving obstacles using deep reinforcement learning. The project connects robot navigation, policy learning, and safety in human-populated spaces.

It also anchors my longer-term interest in robots that can coexist with humans in dynamic environments.
